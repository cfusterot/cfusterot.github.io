Coral Fustero-Torre is a computational biologist and researcher. Her work investigates the molecular and cellular systems that shape life, using advanced computational methods to reveal hidden structures and dynamics within them. She specializes in single-cell and long-read sequencing technologies, mitochondrial lineage tracing, and the development of bespoke analytical tools to explore biological complexity. Alongside her scientific work, she explores analog photography and Super8 filmmaking, reflecting a multidisciplinary approach to life and technology.

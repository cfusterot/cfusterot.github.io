Coral Fustero-Torre, PhD

Computational Biologist & Visual Artist

Coral Fustero-Torre is a computational biologist and researcher. Her work investigates the molecular and cellular systems that shape life, using advanced computational methods to reveal hidden structures and dynamics within them. She specializes in single-cell sequencing technologies and the development of analytical tools to decipher biological complexity and evolution. Alongside her scientific work, she explores analog photography, collage and Super8 filmmaking.

Coral Fustero-Torre

Coral Fustero-Torre is a computational biologist and researcher. Her work investigates complex molecular and cellular systems using advanced computational methods to reveal hidden structures and dynamics within them. She specializes in single-cell sequencing technologies and the development of analytical tools to decipher biological complexity and evolution. Alongside her scientific work, she explores analog photography, collage and filmmaking.
